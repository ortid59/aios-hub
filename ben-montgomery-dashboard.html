MODULE 01

Lead Tracking & CRM

MODULE 02
📄

Proposal Generation

You finish a discovery call, feed the system your notes, and get a fully drafted proposal in minutes. Not hours. You review, adjust, and send. The PowerPoint template becomes a starting point the AI fills.

You said: "I plug in all the information I gather from a sales call into a formatted PowerPoint."
Replaces: Manual PowerPoint
MODULE 03
📋

Automated Quarterly Reviews

The system pulls from Ninja, Grafana, Element, and Power BI automatically, drafts the executive deck, and queues it for your review. Eight to ten clients per year — all handled without manual data assembly.

You said: "I pull from Ninja, Grafana, Element, Power BI and put it into a deck. This is all manual process."
Replaces: 4 manual data pulls
MODULE 04
🏗️

Project Hours & SOW Tracker

Real-time view of every active project — hours used vs. budgeted, scope status, and an automatic alert when you're approaching the 25% overage threshold before the client gets surprised by the invoice.

You said: "If it's a project that we figure 10 hours on, I need to know if we're approaching that 10 hours and we're not completing the project."
Replaces: Manual check-ins
MODULE 05
🖥️

Unified Role Dashboard

One screen. Pipeline, client health, open tickets, project status, and follow-up queue — all visible at once. You stop getting pulled into P1 calls blind because you can see what's escalating before it becomes a call.

You said: "We don't have any type of live dashboard. They just call me when it gets to a point where I need to reach out to someone."
Replaces: Reactive Teams pings

🤖

Follow-Up Automation

Every proposal that goes out has a follow-up sequence behind it. Day 3, day 7, day 14 — customized messages based on where the prospect is in the conversation. Proposals stop going cold because no one had time to follow up.

You said: "A lot of times they get caught up in other things. I don't want to say I'm being ghosted, but proposals stall."
