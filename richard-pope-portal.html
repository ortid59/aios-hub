💡 Why Dollar Cost Averaging Works for You

Removes Timing Risk

By investing $7,000 every month regardless of market conditions, you automatically buy more shares when prices are low and fewer when prices are high.

Bear Markets = Buying Opportunity

When markets drop, your fixed contribution buys more units at a discount. This lowers your average cost and turbocharges your recovery. See the simulator below.

Discipline Beats Prediction

No one consistently times the market. DCA enforces discipline — you stay invested through ups and downs, letting compounding work uninterrupted over your time horizon.
