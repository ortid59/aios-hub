Proposal Generation

Finish a discovery call, feed the system your notes, and get a full draft in minutes. You review, adjust, and send. Follow-up sequences run automatically behind every proposal sent.

Ben said: "I plug all the information into a formatted PowerPoint. Proposals stall — I don't want to say I'm being ghosted, but..."
Replaces: Manual PowerPoint
MODULE 03
📋

Automated Quarterly Reviews

Ninja, Grafana, Element, and Power BI pull automatically. The executive deck gets drafted and queued for your review. Eight to ten clients a year — no manual assembly.

Ben said: "I pull from all these reporting systems and put it into a deck. This is all manual process."
Replaces: 4 manual data pulls
MODULE 04
🏗️

Project Hours & SOW Tracker

Live view of every project against budget. Automatic alert at 75% of hours before the client gets surprised. SOWs drafted from project notes — ready for review and sign-off.

Ben said: "I need to know if we're approaching that 10 hours and we're not completing the project."
Replaces: Manual check-ins
MODULE 05
🖥️

Unified Role Dashboard

One screen: pipeline, client health, escalating tickets, project hours, and follow-up queue. You see P1s before they become a Teams ping. Reactive becomes proactive.

Ben said: "We don't have any live dashboard. They just call me when it gets to a point where I need to reach out."
Replaces: Reactive Teams pings
